Turn any Amazon link into a fully-optimized eBay listing.
Why Use the AI Auto-Lister?

Manually creating eBay listings takes hours and leaves money on the table. Dropanion’s AI Auto-Lister lets you paste a single Amazon URL—or upload a list of them—and automatically generates SEO-optimized titles, descriptions, and photos for each item.

How the AI Auto-Lister Works
  1. Paste Your Amazon Link(s): Copy the product URL(s) from Amazon and paste them into Dropanion’s Auto-Lister dashboard.
  2. Instant Listing Creation: Dropanion fetches all details, then uses AI to craft titles, write descriptions, and pull high-quality photos. Listings are auto-checked for VeRO compliance.
  3. Set Profit & Preferences: Set your profit, shipping, and options for each batch or individual listing.
  4. Publish to eBay: With one click, send your new listings live to your eBay store—no manual work required.
Key Benefits
  • Bulk list hundreds of items at once—save hours
  • SEO-friendly titles and descriptions generated automatically
  • Built-in VeRO checker for compliance
  • No API connection needed
