How It Works

Three simple steps to automate your eBay business

1. Product Research

Dropanion scans top eBay sellers' storefronts to find their best-selling, highest-margin products. It then automatically pulls in the matching Amazon supplier links so you can instantly relist proven winners in your own store.

2. AI Listing Automation

Once a winning product is identified, Dropanion's Auto-Lister uses AI-optimized titles, descriptions, and built-in VERO checks to publish hundreds—or even thousands—of listings automatically. No manual work needed.

3. Smart Order Fulfillment

Once an item sells, Smart Fulfillment matches your eBay listing back to its Amazon source link and returns the exact supplier URL for hassle-free fulfillment.
